About

Welcome to Jamaat-e-Islami, an influential Islamic organization committed to fostering justice, social equity, and spiritual growth. Established with a vision to uphold the principles of Islam in the modern world, our mission is to guide individuals and communities towards a harmonious and balanced society based on Islamic teachings.

Jamaat-e-Islami was founded by Syed Abul Ala Maududi in 1941. A visionary Islamic scholar, Maududi's profound understanding of Islamic principles and his commitment to social reform were the driving forces behind the establishment of our organization. His intellectual contributions, including his extensive writings and speeches, have had a lasting impact on the Islamic thought and the socio-political landscape of the Muslim world.
Jamaat-e-Islami operates through a network of dedicated volunteers, activists, and professionals who are passionate about making a positive impact. Our activities span a range of areas including education, healthcare, social welfare, and advocacy. We work collaboratively with local and international partners to address pressing global issues and to bring about meaningful change.

Our Mission

Social Justice: We are dedicated to advocating for the rights of the marginalized and working towards eliminating social inequalities. Our initiatives aim to create a fair and just society where everyone has equal opportunities and access to resources.
Educational Excellence: We believe in the transformative power of education. Through our educational programs and institutions, we strive to provide high-quality education that not only enriches minds but also nurtures character and ethical values.
Community Development: Engaging with communities to address their needs and challenges is at the heart of our work. We support various development projects that enhance living standards and build sustainable futures for individuals and families.
Spiritual Growth: We encourage personal and collective growth through spiritual development. Our programs and activities are designed to help individuals deepen their faith and lead lives that reflect the principles of Islam.
